About Us

CJ Foods, Inc. is the U.S. food operations headquarters for CJ Cheiljedang. Since its opening in 1978, CJ Foods has quickly become a major manufacturer and distributor of Asian food products with a focus on Korean food. Through our brands, Bibigo and Annie Chun's, we are able to offer an extensive range of packaged food products, including dumplings, noodles and sauces. Backed by a global company with over 60 years of experience in the food industry, CJ Foods’ mission is to drive awareness of Asian food, especially Korean food, and provide only the best quality products to U.S. households across the nation.

CJ FOODS, INC

To expand the globalization of Korean food to the Americas, CJ started its first U.S. food operations in Southern California and has quickly become a major manufacturer and distributor of Asian food products with a focus on Korean food. Through its brands, Bibigo and Annie Chun's, CJ Foods is able to offer an extensive range of packaged food products, including dumplings, noodles and sauces.

CJ Cheiljedang

CJ Cheiljedang has been leading Korea's food industry for 60 years. It spreads the excellence of Korean flavors to every corner of the world with K-Food, a cuisine that satisfies local tastes with a touch of Korean flavor. CJ Cheiljedang supports healthy and convenient lifestyles by offering a variety of premium products that are created with the newest technologies, and contributes to a tastier and healthier world by developing food products made with ingredients from nature. CJ Cheiljedang spreads Korean taste and culture around the world through continuous research, development, progression and innovation.
